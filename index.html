What is Gracie?

We are a global happiness index, building on Ethereum. We pay our users to tell us how they are feeling and allow them to monetize their emotional data through our network of content providers, merchants and satisfaction markets.

“Asking people whether they are happy, or satisfied with their lives, offers important information about the society. It can signal underlying crises or hidden strengths. It can suggest the need for change.”

- Jeffrey Sachs, Columbia University
