GRACE Tokens power Gracie's happiness platform

Gracie utilizes a duo-token economic model, GRACE for Governance and HELP for Rewards. GRACE is a staking token which allows organizations and individuals to have a say in how we develop the platform and to generate HELP tokens.

HELP tokens are a tokenized reward for interacting with the platform and used as a fee for transacting on the platform.
